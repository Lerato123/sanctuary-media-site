The Eye Behind the Lens

"I don't just take photographs — I create the images people keep on their walls for decades."

Njabulo is the creative force behind Sanctuary Media — a Johannesburg-based photography and media production studio dedicated to telling authentic visual stories. With a sharp eye for light, emotion, and composition, Njabulo brings a cinematic quality to every shoot.

From intimate portraits to large-scale commercial campaigns, Sanctuary Media delivers work that resonates — images that don't just document moments, but define them.
